About this project


This original idea started as a joke internally on our team. Mid-way though some of our first pair-programming exercises we noticed that just having a partner there made the debugging much easier. In fact, that partner usually sat silently while the one in the driver’s seat explained what he was doing. We began referring to pair programming as “Rubber Ducking.” I even teased my VP of engineering as being my favorite Rubber Duck. As a surprise for our summer company retreat, we created a virtual rubber duck, one that you could summon from the stroke of a couple of keys.
