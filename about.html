About

SimpleCTO is a way for me to better organize my thoughts, share some insights, and publicly air some questions to anyone passing by. Now, I know what you are thinking:

We need another blog like I need another asshole.
– Yours truly

But hear me out. Because I am not your typical asshole.

You see, this is not my first rodeo. I’ve been in the game for a while which means there are some scars to go along with my successes. I’ve had the pleasure and pain of working with some colorful characters, in colorful places, and in some colorful situations. The names have been changed to protect the sinners, the saints, and those still in purgatory. Maybe by sharing them we can all take something away. Either way, I hope you get a good laugh out of it.

So, who the hell am I?

Good question. Currently I am CTO at NuvoAir where I lead a growing team of data-scientists, mobile, and back-end developers to deliver digital health solutions in the respiratory space.

I’ve been fortunate enough in my career to have the freedom to explore new ideas and opportunities across e-commerce, advertising, startups, and healthcare. In that time I was a developer, copywriter, manager, and CTO (among other fill-in roles).
